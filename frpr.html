Privacy Policy

At FoodRisk, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Food Data: Images and information about food items you scan or input
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, features used, and time spent
  • Optional Account Information: If you create an account, we collect email address and username

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide food analysis and health risk assessments
  • Improve and optimize our application
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Send important updates and notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research or statistical purposes. We may share data with service providers who help us operate our application, but they are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Camera and Photo Library Access

Our app requires access to your device's camera and photo library to scan food items. These permissions are only used for the purpose of food analysis and are not used to collect personal images unrelated to this purpose.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection
  • Export your data in a common format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@foodrisk.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at chefai.app@gmail.com.
